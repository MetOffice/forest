An Interactive Explorer for Madden-Julian Oscillation (MJO)

Interact with the widgets on the left to query a date for which you will be able to see the MJO RMM indices and their forecasts in GloSea5 and MOGREPS systems. Grey lines are the analysis and sky blue coloured lines are individual member forecasts. Ensemble mean is plotted as thicker blue lines. You may place the mouse over the lines to see more information on the plots.

(Plotted using Bokeh)
